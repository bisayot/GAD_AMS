# Budget Concepts — GAD-IMS Interview Notes (June 22)

---

## Budget Input Rules

- Only the budget that **GAD will fund** is entered in the digital form — not the full proposed budget, since some ADs are co-funded by other offices.
- **No exceeding the proposed budget** is allowed at the point of submission.
- AD revisions are still permitted even when actual spending exceeds the proposed budget (to reflect the updated figures).
- The proposed budget limit must still be set regardless.

---

## Budget Auto-Computation

Computation varies depending on the **venue type** (inside or outside BSU).

### Meals

**Formula:** Cost × Target Participants × Frequency per day (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)

| Venue | Rate |
|---|---|
| Inside BSU | ₱220 per meal |
| Outside BSU | ₱350 per meal |

### Snacks

| Venue | Rate |
|---|---|
| Inside BSU | ₱80 per person |
| Outside BSU | ₱150 per person |

> **Note:** Coffee is NOT included in snacks — it should be listed under "Others."

> **Note:** Minimum of **50 participants** required for GAD to fund an activity.

### Special Instances (Outside Venue — Food & Accommodation Included) — Per Pax per Day

| Location | Rate |
|---|---|
| Within CAR | ₱1,500 – ₱2,100 |
| Within NCR | ₱1,100 |

## Budget Line Items

### Function Room / Venue
- Usually free when held **within BSU** (attribution basis).
- If **outside**: approximately ₱1,000 per hour.
  - Reference: resort in Asin; typical usage is 8–10 hours per day.

### Accommodation
- Applicable **only for outside venues**.
- Rate: **₱900 per night per pax**.

### Equipment Rental
- Typically free or bundled as a package.
- Still included as a line item for cases where it applies.

### Professional Fee / Honoraria
- Rate: **₱2,258.255** (rounded to ₱2,300).

### Tokens
- Rate: **₱1,000 per person**.

### Materials and Supplies
- Rate: **₱1,000 per participant**.

### Transportation
- Maximum cap: **₱20,000**.

### Others
- A catch-all line item for expenses not covered by the above (e.g., coffee).

## Budget Flow / Computation Logic

### Step-by-Step Flow

**Step 1 — AD Proposal Submission**
TWG submits the Activity Design Proposal to GAD, which includes the Budget Proposal Details in the form.

**Step 2 — GAD Director Review & Approval**
The GAD Director reviews the AD. If **approved**:
```
Total Allocated Budget  −= Total Budget Proposed
Total Pending Budget    += Total Budget Proposed
```

**Step 3 — Accomplishment Report Submission**
Once the activity is done, the TWG submits an Accomplishment Report (AR) which includes the Actual Budget Spending Breakdown. When the AR is submitted:
```
Total Pending Budget (of that AD)  −= Total Actual Budget Spent (from AR)
Difference                          = Total Pending Budget − Total Actual Budget Spent

Total Available Budget  += Difference          (unspent amount goes back)
Total Utilized Amount   += Total Actual Budget Spent
```

### Summary of Budget Buckets

| Budget Bucket | When It Changes |
|---|---|
| **Total Allocated Budget** | Decreases when an AD is approved |
| **Total Pending Budget** | Increases on AD approval; decreases on AR submission |
| **Total Available Budget** | Increases when AR is submitted (receives the unspent difference) |
| **Total Utilized Amount** | Increases when AR is submitted (receives the actual spending) |

## Mandate Budget Concerns

- GAD Plan allocated mandate budgets are **editable** — plans can change within the same fiscal year.
- Informal limit of **₱15,000 per college** for budget allocation, but this is not strictly enforced since unused funds from other colleges would otherwise go to waste.

---

## Activity Design (AD) — Budget-Related Rules

- ADs can be **revised** even when ongoing or completed — particularly when actual spending exceeds the proposed budget (TWGs must update the proposed budget to match).
- No hard limit on the number of activities a TWG can propose or implement.
- ADs are submitted **at least 2 weeks before the start date** and must fall within the proposed year.
- Rush proposals may be accepted at the director's discretion.
